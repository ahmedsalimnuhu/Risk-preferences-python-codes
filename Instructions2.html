RULES (continued):

  • As in the example of candies, the questions deal with whether you prefer to have a smaller amount of money for sure OR a larger amount of money where there is some risk that you end up not getting anything. You can never lose any money irrespective of what you choose.

    For example, consider the following two options:
    • Option A: You get 200 KSH for sure.
    • Option B: We will flip a coin. If it lands on heads you get 500 KSH. If it lands on tails you get nothing.

  • If you choose Option A, you know you get 200 KSH for sure.  If you choose Option B, where we flip a coin, you might get 500 KSH, but there is also the same exact chance you will get nothing. That is you will get 0.

  • We will show you {{n}} questions like these but with different amounts of money. These differences are important.

  • Remember, the best you can do is to think carefully about each question as if it were the only choice you make.
